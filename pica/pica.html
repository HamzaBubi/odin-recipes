Pizza recipe

This is a simple pizza recipe:

Ingredients for this dish are:

  • 2&1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1/4 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1&1/2 cups of pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
